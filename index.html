Spin A Web

Dates : 26th & 27th March 2016
  • Rules
  • Contact

  • A team of 2 members should be formed.
  • There will be two rounds.
  • The first round will be of 45 mins where the team may sketch the layout of their webpage.
  • They will be asked to explain the working of teh webpage and the transition of each elements.
  • 15 to 20 teams will qualify for round 2 which would go for 2 hours.
  • The coding should be done in HTML, CSS and Javascript. No templates allowed.
  • Sublime, Notepad++, Atom or Brackets are the accepted IDE's to import the Jscript Packages.

Paper Dropbox

Dates : 26th & 27th March 2016
  • Rules
  • Contact

  • Participants must be present at the venue 10 mins before the commencement of the event. Late comers will not be entertained.
  • Participants should be dressed in formals.
  • Participants may present for a total time of 10 mins. There will be a warning bell at the end of 7 mins. Exceeding time limit may lead to negative marking.
  • Topic chosen must be purely 'technical' and should be yield minimum of 50 results on browsing. Topic should be belong to given Computer Science domain and should not be vague.
  • There will be Question & Answer session following the presentation in which the participant is expected to answer questions posted by judges.
  • Presentations aids such as powerpoint presentation is accepted. Videos, graphics or animation content should not be be present.
  • Weightage is given to presentation and communication skills of participants.
