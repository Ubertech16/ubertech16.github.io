Android Workshop by DesignX Club

Dates : 26th & 27th August 2016
  • Details
  • Contact

DesignX, a club by the CSE department, is having an Android development workshop on 26th and 27th August. It is Rs.300 per person (including OD, lunch and certificates). You will learn about the basic design principles and app development on the first day of the workshop and implement it for the next day's competition which has a cash prize of Rs.3000 for the winners and Rs.1500 for the runner-up.
